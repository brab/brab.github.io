Ben Rabinovitch

I'm a web software developer with a Bachelor of Computing Sciences from Simon Fraser University in Burnaby British Columbia. I have had the pleasure of working on small, agile teams since 2009 to produce fantastic products and user-experiences with high quality code that I, and the teams I've worked with, are very proud of. I believe in constant and confident shipping of product supported by Test Driven Development and automation.

I am currently the Lead Software Engineer at Eventsage where we build tools to make everyone a professional event planner. It has been my responsibility since the beginning of the project to ensure that we have a well-designed tool that is both intuitive for our users as well as easy to maintain and extend for new functionality into the foreseeable future.
In 2012 when we began development we decided that AngularJS would be one of the foundational technologies of our product despite its very recent development and we haven't regretted the choice once. It has proven to be a well-designed framework that fits our needs perfectly.
We also chose to work with Flask to build our back-end API, and while it's been fun to work with, the challenges and limitations of it have made it a less than an ideal choice.
